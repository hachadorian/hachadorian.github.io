About Me:

Hi my name is Tyler Hachadorian and I am a senior at Temple University studying Information Science and Technology. Originally I planned on going to Temple for Music Technology. I always wanted to have a career in scoring video games and possibly movies as well. Unfortunately, that did not pan out however, I am extremely excited and motivated with my current studies as well. I think some of the creativity that comes along with producing music helps me a lot in the problem solving process and being forced to find a creative solution to problems is one of the reasons I chose this career path.

I think generally I am a fast learner and am able to pick things up very quickly. For example, I can play the piano, guitar, and bass which I largely taught myself. I also have experience in a number of different Adobe applications which I also taught myself. I enjoy picking up new things and when I find myself enjoying something I can spend a large amount of time on that thing and not think twice about it.

Growing up I also played a number of different team sports but primarily ice hockey. I still love to play hockey and unfortunately, I am a very passionate Flyers fan. I think my experience with team sports has taught me a ton about working in a team to achieve a common goal.
